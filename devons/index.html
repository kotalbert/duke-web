Devon Rex Cats

About

This web page is dedicated to the Devon Rex Cats.

Devon Rex is a relatively new cat breed, descending from the UK. It was discovered in 1960s, when a strange looking stray cat was found in Devon County.

Because of they peculiar look, they have earned many nicknames.

They share manny similarities with the other short haired cat breeds like Cornish Rex and Sphynx, because of their similar genetic makeup.

They are considered to be one of the most inteligent cat breeds and poses friendly personality. They get very attached to their owners and look for their constant attention.
